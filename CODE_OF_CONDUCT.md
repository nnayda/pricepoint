# Code of Conduct

This project adopts the
[Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

In short: be respectful, be constructive, and assume good faith. Harassment,
personal attacks, and derailing behavior are not tolerated.

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the maintainer at
ntnayda@gmail.com. All reports will be reviewed and handled confidentially.
